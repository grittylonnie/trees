HELP REGROW URBAN GREEN SPACE

The death of trees in vulnerable communities.

When walking around Philie’s most vulnerable neighborhoods in the dead heat of summer, it’s not hard to miss the lack of trees. It is not uncommon to hear residents mention how there were more trees when they were younger vs current times. According to phillytrees between 2008 and 2018 Philly lost roughly 1,095 acres of tree canopy.
